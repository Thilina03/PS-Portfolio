Lecture 06 - RESEARCH WRITING

Background of the Study

Managing language policy in Sri Lanka.

Research Problem

Language policies in educational settings and impact on students’ learning — with a focus on the Sri Lankan state university context.

Research Objectives

Investigate the implementation of language planning and policy in higher education at the Faculty of Management and Finance, University of Ruhuna.

Literature Review

Previous studies have explored language policies in educational settings and their impact on students’ learning.

Significance

  • Contributes to deeper understanding of how language policies affect educational outcomes in a multilingual environment.
