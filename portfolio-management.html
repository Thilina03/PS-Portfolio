Types of Portfolios

PERSONAL PORTFOLIO

A Personal Portfolio is a collection of work, achievements, and projects that showcase your skills, creativity, and unique style commonly used in fields like art, design, and media.

CAREER/DOSSIER PORTFOLIO

A Career Portfolio showcases your professional achievements, including a resume, cover letter, references, and work experience used for job applications, promotions, or education.

EMPLOYMENT PORTFOLIO

To demonstrate your worth and foster professional development, an Employment Portfolio is a compilation of records and samples of your work that showcase your abilities, accomplishments, and contributions.

ASSESSMENT PORTFOLIO

An Assessment Portfolio showcases learning progress and achievements over time, often used in education to demonstrate subject understanding and support academic evaluation.

  • Define Your Purpose

  • Choose Your Format
  • Collect Your Materials
  • Organize Your Portfolio
  • Provide Context
  • Regularly Update Your Portfolio
  • Seek Feedback

  • Protect Your Privacy
